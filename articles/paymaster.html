PayMaster

The PayMaster feature in Holdstation Wallet provides the flexibility to pay gas fees using tokens other than the native token of the blockchain (e.g., ETH, BNB). Supported tokens include HOLD, ZK, uGOLD, USDC, USDC.e, and USDT.

How to Use PayMaster for Swaps

1. Open the Holdstation Wallet app and navigate to the swap feature.

2. Select the token you wish to swap and enter the desired amount.

3. Below the amount field, locate the small box with a fee icon.

4. Tap the fee icon to choose the token you want to use for the gas fee (e.g., HOLD, USDC).

5. Complete the swap as usual. The gas fee will be deducted from the selected token.

How to Use PayMaster for Sending Tokens

1. Open the Holdstation Wallet app and initiate a send transaction.

2. Select the recipient wallet address and enter the amount of tokens you want to send.

3. Below the amount field, find the small box with a fee icon.

4. Tap the fee icon to choose the token to use for paying the gas fee.

5. Confirm the transaction. The gas fee will be deducted from the chosen token.

By using PayMaster, users can manage transactions even when their native token balance is low, offering a more seamless experience.
