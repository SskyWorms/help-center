How to Withdraw Crypto from Holdstation Wallet

Open Holdstation Wallet: Launch the app and ensure you're on the correct blockchain network (zkSync, BeraChain, Ethereum, ...). You can select the network by tapping the chain selector at the top of the main screen.

Locate the Withdrawal Feature: Tap on the "Send" button, usually found on the wallet's home screen.

Enter Recipient's Address: Paste the address where you want to send the crypto (this could be an exchange address, another wallet, etc.). Always double-check that the address is correct and matches the network you're sending on.

Select the Token: Choose the cryptocurrency you wish to withdraw.

Enter the Amount: Specify the amount of crypto you want to send. Ensure you have enough balance, including any network (gas) fees.

Review the Transaction: Double-check the recipientâ€™s address, the amount, and the network fee before confirming.

Confirm the Transaction: Enter your PIN code or confirm via fingerprint or FaceID to complete the transaction.

Wait for Confirmation: After submitting, the transaction will be processed by the blockchain. You can track its status within the app or on a blockchain explorer.
