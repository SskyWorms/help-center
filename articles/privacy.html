How to View Your Private Key

Open Holdstation Wallet: Launch the app and ensure you're on the correct blockchain network (zkSync, BeraChain, Ethereum, ...). You can select the network by tapping the chain selector at the top of the main screen.

Open the Holdstation App and navigate to the Explore tab.

Tap on "Security & Privacy".

From there, select "View Seed Phrase / Private Key".

You will be asked to enter your PIN code or use biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint or face scan) for security purposes.

Once authenticated, your Private Key will be revealed.

Important: Store your Private Key safely, and never share it with anyone, as it provides full access to your wallet.
