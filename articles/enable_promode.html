How to Enable Pro Mode

Open the Holdstation App and navigate to the Explore tab.

In the Explore tab of the app, find and tap on "Pro Mode".

Pro Mode enables faster transactions, including a 1-click transaction feature.

Additionally, you can set an option to receive notifications or take action when the gas fee exceeds [user-specified amount] ETH.
